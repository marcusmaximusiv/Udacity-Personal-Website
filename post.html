TYLER HERRO

THE NEXT MIAMI HEAT STAR?

Name: Mark Sterrenberg

Title: Sports Columnist

Company: CBS Sports

Tyler Herro has recently come off the bench and has had, since October 25th, a game where he has scored 10 points or more which we haven't seen from a player coming off the bench in an extremely long time since James Harden back when he came off the bench for the Oklahoma City Thunder.

This season especially he has been amazing at field goal percentage jumping from 43.9% from the field this past season to 46% which means he is finding his spots better and putting the ball in the basket when they count.

With 39.3% 3-point spot Herro is a danger from distance and that puts a lot of pressure on opponents because he is versatile as a shooter and off the ball offensive weapon as well like he showed at his days at Memphis University. Each year his FG% and 3PT% get better and better to the point where i think he will be a superstar talent in no time.
